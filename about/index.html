About CC



Capital Cubing is the most prominent Cubing Organization in India, aimed at improving the quality of cubing competitions in the country. We provide a platform for anybody who wanted to organize a competition but could not because of guidance problems or lack of an organizing team by working with our team. This is for everyone who was disturbed by some things they saw in competitions they attended and wanted to fix them. We make sure to provide you the platform and resources.

If you've been solving that Rubik's Cube for a while, and would like to compete, take a look at our upcoming competitions pages. If you're new to competing, see our Competitor Guide so you know what's in store for you when you go to a competition. Use the contact form below to reach out to us.
